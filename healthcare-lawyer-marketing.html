Effective Healthcare Attorney Marketing

Healthcare law directly and profoundly affects people’s lives. Without guidance, many will not have the knowledge they need to make the best health care choices for their family. When people are seeking the advice or assistance of a healthcare lawyer, they are generally trying to make difficult decisions, and they are sometimes in need of serious legal help.

The average person today does not always have an understanding of their rights as a patient. The laws and regulations related to the healthcare market are always changing, and generally, people in healthcare crisis are not in a position to do a lot of research on their own to understand these complexities. That’s why they are seeking legal assistance.

But, even then, people do not always know where to turn. In order for you to be able to provide your services to help them in your time of need, your marketing strategy needs to make sure that you are there for them at that exact moment that they are looking for your services. Unlike man products and services that are sold on the internet, there is a very limited and distinct window when you can be there for your client. You need an effective and efficient marketing plan in order to reach them when needed. If your healthcare firm is ready to expand your client base and optimize your ability to find new clients, there are many steps you can take that will be effective.

It can be difficult to know where to start in today’s digital landscape, where you have so many platforms and social media channels to cover. As a healthcare specialist, your practice or firm will want to focus your energies on reaching your target audience. There are several marketing strategies we recommend for our clients. Here are a few basic marketing strategies you can try out:

Optimize!

Those looking for a healthcare attorney through an online search should easily be able to find your law firm or practice among all the other lawyers in your area. You can optimize your healthcare law website to drive more traffic to your practice and increase your healthcare marketing efforts.

When potential clients are searching for a lawyer specializing in healthcare law, most will complete an online search as one of their first steps. If your website does not show up in the first page or pages of their search, they will likely never find you. With consistent work, you can optimize your site so that it is always at the top of the search results when your prospective clients are looking for you.

Create Engaging Content Related to Healthcare

When potential clients do find your healthcare law website online, they will likely spend some time perusing before deciding to make contact. They will want to know how you can help with their health-care-related problem, what services you provide, and about your areas of expertise. The resources and content on your website will tell them a great deal about your attorneys and your firm.

You should use your website to showcase your knowledge in health care law, share resources that would be helpful to those with healthcare problems, and educate your visitors about common (or maybe not-so-common) healthcare-related law issues. They will want to see content that is updated regularly, and that highlights your experience and knowledge in this market. These will provide visitors with confidence in your ability to assist with their healthcare legal needs.

Provide Healthcare Resources in The Community

Another effective marketing strategy is to create and provide some free healthcare resources to members of your community. Create a few informational booklets on pertinent health care issues and the legal ramifications for those in your state, then distribute them at nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, and other relevant locations.

Load an informational video about the medical marijuana laws in your state and post it to your firm’s social media accounts. Get creative by targeting the needs of those in your area, highlighting your healthcare expertise in helpful ways that will bring new clients to your door. Find a need in your area and provide a few, simple resources to fill that need.

Use a Targeted Google Adwords Campaign

When trying to build a client base in a niche such as healthcare law, you want to be sure clients are able to find you when searching for terms related to your practice. The number of people who click on sponsored links in a Google search is rising, and a campaign such as one using Adwords can be very efficient and effective. It is important that the agency is helping you run your Adwords campaign understands this tool and the use of SEO and other strategies (like we do!) to make the most of this effort.

In addition, re-targeting campaigns can also assist you in increasing your conversion rate. When the average person searches for a healthcare attorney, they take multiple days to decide if they want to hire you or not. One they have visited your website, re-targeting ads will appear in their feed, reminding them of your law practice’s expertise and knowledge as they browse the internet. If you are already utilizing an Adwords campaign and have organic SEO for your site, then re-targeting is an easy and efficient strategy.

Watch what other firms are doing

One of the best things you can do to effectively market your legal services is to look at what your competitors are doing to market their services in the same field. You want to monitor their strate-gies and their progress with those strategies. For example, take a look at where they are investing in Google keywords and AdWords. However, you don’t want to just copy what they’re doing. Doing that merely drives up the cost of marketing.

Lawyers have a habit of falling into a copycat trap. It’s easy, as a lawyer, to find an argument that works and merely repeat it, hammer it home over and over again. In the law, precedent is king. But that’s not how it works in marketing. In marketing, you need to differentiate yourself in order to give your law firm and its services more visibility. You need to be different to stand out from the crowd.

You need to see what your competitors are doing but then do it better. You need to see what keywords they are using, but then make sure that your efforts are more focused than theirs. By seeing what keywords they are using, you can see clients you shouldn’t be targeting because they wouldn’t benefit from your services or you could find a whole class of clients that are perfect for you, but everyone else is overlooking.

But don’t overanalyze. Walk away. Don’t waste your time constantly monitoring what everyone else is doing. Then there will be no time for you to market your own business let alone provide the valuable legal representation that you specialize in. Get the information that you need, then incorporate it into your multifaceted marketing plan. After some time passes, return to monitoring their marketing efforts to see what they’ve done to change their efforts. If you’ve been success-ful in your own efforts, they’ll likely have started copying you. In which case, you can then keep your own marketing campaign efficient and effective by making some alterations and adapting your efforts to better focus on your target client. Once that’s done rinse and repeat as needed.

Do not fall in line with your competition. Distinguish yourself and drive traffic to your website. Once everyone else is copying you, adapt, revise, and then rinse and repeat as needed. Let every-one else fall in line behind you.

Measure What You are Doing (So You Know What’s Working)

There is not much point in engaging in marketing or advertising efforts for your health care attorneys if you cannot see which efforts are successful. Identifying from where your new leads are being generated is an important metric for any marketing campaign. Make sure that any Adwords campaign, outreach attempts, or other marketing strategies have measurable outcomes that can provide you with actionable data. Your marketing strategy should be one that allows you to invest more in areas that are yielding new leads and eliminate those that are not.

Make Sure Your Firm Is Being Reviewed

Another way to be sure you are placing high in Google searches and on social media platforms is to have excellent reviews. When your attorneys are ranked highly in Google, your firm or practice will appear near the top of the results on Google Maps.

Positive reviews on Yelp, Facebook, Google, and other platforms are important to potential clients, particularly those looking for assistance with a niche law problem such as healthcare. Your potential clients will look at your reviews on these platforms, and if your health care lawyers do not have at least an 80% satisfaction rating, those potential clients will go elsewhere. Encourage your current and past clients to rate your healthcare lawyers.

Maximize Your Intake Process

Whether new and potential clients reach out through email, the phone, or a web-based form, you must be able to respond to all inquiries quickly, with care and understanding, and in a professional manner. It is still surprising how often client inquiries go unanswered, how long it takes for phone calls to be returned, or for clients to be greeted by a less-than-friendly voice on the other end of the line.

All of these opportunities for intake are also opportunities to turn potential clients into actual clients. When people call or contact your health care law office with a question or concern, they want reassurance that you care. Without that, they will move on. This simple strategy is one that can yield great dividends.
